About Grace Church

Discover our story, our mission, and the people who make Grace Church a welcoming home for all.

Our History

House of Christ was founded in 2013 by our current overseer Pastor Kenneth Achuenu and Pst Mrs Rose Achuenu Ogbehon in Verona, Italy. Since then, the ministry has flourished, and we have expanded our mission to bring the message of our Lord Jesus Christ to the UK. The church in Italy is now growing at a rapid rate, and we have decided to move to Newcastle, United Kingdom, to continue spreading the message of our Lord Jesus Christ.

Pastor Kenneth Achuenu

Our Objectives

  • Community Outreach: Engage with local communities to spread the love of Christ through service and support.
  • Spiritual Growth: Foster an environment where individuals can deepen their relationship with God and grow in their faith.
  • Global Impact: Extend our ministry beyond borders to reach and touch lives around the world with the message of hope and salvation.
