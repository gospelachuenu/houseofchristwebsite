Our History

From Italy to the UK — A Ministry on the Move

House of Christ was founded in 2013 by Pastor Kenneth Achuenu and Pst Mrs Rose Achuenu Ogbehon in Verona, Italy. What began as a small gathering of believers has flourished into a growing international ministry.

With the Italian church continuing to grow at a rapid rate, God opened a door to expand the mission to Newcastle, United Kingdom — bringing the message of our Lord Jesus Christ to a new city and a new generation.

Today, House of Christ International serves two thriving congregations — one in Verona, Italy, and one in Newcastle, UK — united by one vision: to make disciples, reach the nations, and glorify God.
